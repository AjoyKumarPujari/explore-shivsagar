Demographics

An official Census 2011 detail of Sivasagar

                                In 2011, Sivasagar had population of 1,151,050 of which male and female were 589,216 and 561,834 respectively. In 2001 census, Sivasagar had a population of 1,051,736 of which males were 545,476 and remaining 506,260 were females. Sivasagar District population constituted 3.69 percent of total Maharashtra population. In 2001 census, this figure for Sivasagar District was at 3.95 percent of Maharashtra population.
                                There was change of 9.44 percent in the population compared to population as per 2001. In the previous census of India 2001, Sivasagar District recorded increase of 15.83 percent to its population compared to 1991. Average literacy rate of Sivasagar in 2011 were 80.41 compared to 74.47 of 2001. If things are looked out at gender wise, male and female literacy were 85.84 and 74.71 respectively. For 2001 census, same figures stood at 81.53 and 66.81 in Sivasagar District. Total literate in Sivasagar District were 813,505 of which male and female were 444,767 and 368,738 respectively. In 2001, Sivasagar District had 7,965,351 in its district. Most of the inhabitants of the town are Assamese, comprising Ahom, Koch, Assamese Hindus, Assamese Muslims, and other ethnic groups.
