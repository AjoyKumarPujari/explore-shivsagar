Tour Planning

Sivsagar in summer(From April till July):
Sivasagar(Assam) is refreshing in the summer season; the weather doesn't get too hot. Summer stays on for a shorter time span (March- June). The maximum temperature reaches about 37 degrees Celsius. Whenever it becomes too hot, rain showers come to the rescue. This is a perfect time to explore and visit its attractions like Rang Ghar, Tolatol Ghor, Kareng Ghor , Siva Dol, Devi Dol etc.
Sivasagar in Monsoon(From July till September):
Sivasagar (Assam) receives abundant rainfall in the monsoon months. The monsoon season starts off with the month of June and lasts till October. Try to avoid visiting Assam in this season, as heavy showers often result in landslides, also the National Parks and Sanctuaries Remain close during monsoon.
Sivasagar in winter(From October till March)
Winters of Sivasagar (Assam) are chilling but enlivening. The area gets foggy and light rain showers around. Lowest temperature in winters is about 6 degrees Celsius. This is a good time to visit Assam.
